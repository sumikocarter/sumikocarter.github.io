About me

Hello!
I am the Design Director and UI/UX Practice lead at Gorilla Logic, a software consulting company in Boulder, CO.

Perviously, I worked at at Threespot, an NGO focused digital agency in Washington, DC. I primarily contributed to Peace Corps, Planned Parenthood, and the National Park Service team.

I joined Threespot in September 2011 from U.S. News & World Report. While at U.S. News, I was a key player in the redesign of usnews.com and an integral part of the launch of several high-profile products. Earlier in my career, I worked as a designer for National Geographic International Publishing Group and as a Math/Science Education Volunteer for the Peace Corps in rural Kenya.

As a BFA graduate of Middlebury College and Boston University Center for Digital Imaging Arts, I am grateful that my passions have taken me around the world. When not in the studio, I can be found down at the sand volleyball courts, exploring the outdoors or day dreaming of upgrading my household appliances.
